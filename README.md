Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis (Python – Pandas & Seaborn)
# Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance using real-world KPIs such as:

CTR – Click Through Rate

CVR – Conversion Rate

CPC – Cost Per Click

CPA – Cost Per Acquisition

ROAS – Return on Ad Spend

The goal is to evaluate campaign efficiency and identify the highest-performing marketing strategies.

# Example Insights

“Black Friday” campaign delivers the highest revenue.

“Retargeting” has a strong conversion rate despite lower clicks.

“Holiday Promo” achieves a healthy ROAS and stable CVR.
